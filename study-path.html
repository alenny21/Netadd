Study Path

Stop trying to study everything at once.

This roadmap keeps you focused on the highest-value topics first so your studying actually sticks.

Recommended Order

Phase 1

  • OSI model

Infrastructure

  • Switches vs routers

Troubleshooting

  • Subnetting drills
  • Weak-area review
This phase is what pushes people into passing range.
