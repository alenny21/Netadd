Subnetting

Stop fighting the math.

Learn the pattern first. The goal is fast recognition, not perfect textbook math.

The Ladder

Memorize this first

128 → 64 → 32 → 16 → 8 → 4

Shortcut

Usable hosts = jump size minus 2.

Example

Jump size 32 gives you 30 usable hosts.

APIPA

169.254 means DHCP failed.

Quick Practice

Need 6 usable hosts?

Pick /29 because jump size 8 gives 6 usable hosts.

Need 30 usable hosts?

Pick /27 because jump size 32 gives 30 usable hosts.

Need 2 usable hosts?

Pick /30. Common for point-to-point links.

Device has 169.254.x.x?

Think: DHCP problem first.
